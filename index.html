Rathnavali Balika Vidyalaya, is a leading government Buddhist national school in Gampaha, Sri Lanka. Students are admitted to grade six on the basis of results of The Grade 05 Scholarship Examination in Sri Lanka. The school motto is Karotha Buddasaasanan(කරෝථ බුද්ධසාසනං).It is a girls school.
