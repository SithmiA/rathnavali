Badminton

Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. It is a sport for two or four people. The game developed in India during Raj rule from the earlier game of battledore and shuttlecock. Denmark dominates play in Europe in a game that's been very popular in Asia, with competitions usually dominated by China, Indonesia, Singapore and Thailand.
